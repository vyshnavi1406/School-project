Olympiad Exams

To face the National level Competitive Examinations - Academic expertise alone is not sufficient. Keeping this in view, we prepare our students to crack external competitive exams like Olympiads conducted by prestigious organizations at State and National levels in subjects like Hindi, Mathematics, Science, G.K., Computers and English. Attempting these exams will boost their confidence.

Many of our students have topped the merit list in the Olympiads conducted by organizations such as SOF (Science Olympiad foundation), silver zone foundation, TERRI, Unified council, EDU HEAL foundation and many more. In addition to the Olympiads our students have achieved ranks in talent tests such as Sir C.V.Ramana talent test, Ramanajan talent test and many more.

Aditya are found topping the merit lists in almost all the competitive exams they have been enrolling.
